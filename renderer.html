      Audio Settings

      Do not see your device / Device not responding?

      Click the refresh icon in the top right corner of "Audio Settings" to refresh available devices.

      Settings saved automatically

      Settings are saved and applied automatically when you change them; you do not have to restart remote broadcasts nor DJ Controls.

      Audio Devices

      Input Devices

      Capturing computer audio

      To capture computer audio, such as from Skype/Discord/etc, you need to install a program such as Voicemeeter (Windows) or Soundflower (MacOS)... or if your sound card supports it, activate "Stereo Mix".

      Target 50-100% on VU Meters

      Adjust your volume so that the VU meter(s) for your device(s) average between 50% and 100%.

      • If the audio is too loud (> 100%), it could be distorted (but a limiter will be applied to help reduce that)
      • If the audio is too quiet (< 25%) for too long when on the air, the silence detection alarm may trigger

      Output Devices

      One Device Only

      Only one output device may be used for playing remote audio, and one device for audio queues. When you click a checkbox on another device, the checkbox for the previous device will be cleared.

      Audio Queues for Remote Broadcasts

      Currently, audio queue countdowns are played through your default computer audio device and cannot be changed. In a future version, you will be able to choose which device to play queues on.

      Manage Underwritings

      What are Underwritings?

      Underwritings are paid or unpaid sponsorship messages that have a specific date/time schedule, spin count, and/or start and end date to air. Unlike PSAs which air randomly during breaks and have no spin checking, the WWSU system uses complex algorithms to ensure to the best of its ability underwritings air all their spins by the set end date/time (if applicable). The system can also adjust the frequency depending on the number of online listeners currently tuned in, or to air underwritings during specific calendar events. The system will also alert you via system status if it finds it will not be able to air an underwriting the specified number of times before the end date/time.

      Before adding an underwriting here, make sure to import it into RadioDJ in the commercials category, set track type to Commercial, and set the start/end date/time, priority, and number of spins when applicable.

      Please be sure to click "How Underwritings Work" and read the info if you do not yet know how the WWSU system handles underwritings.

      Spin Counts Lifespan

      Spin Counts for RadioDJ tracks are only tallied for the past year, and automatically deleted after 2 years. This is to keep the system running smooth and fast.
